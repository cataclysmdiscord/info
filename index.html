Roles

Staff Roles

@Puppet Master (Owner)

@Puppeteer (Admin)

@Puppet Apprentice (Jr. Admin) - Prove to be a valuable asset as a moderator for some time.

@Puppet Enthusiast (Moderator) - Prove to be an experienced, intelligent, responsible, and enthusiastic member for some amount of time (assuming more mods are needed to handle the server).

@Puppet Prodigy (Jr. Moderator) - Prove to be an active, responsible, and trustworthy member.

To ping all staff members if you need help, please ping @Staff.

*Users are promoted depending on activity and demand for more staff. This means it is possible to meet all staff requirements yet still not receive a promotion.

Other Roles

@Living Puppet (Honored Member) - Be helpful, follow the rules, encourage people to follow them, aid the staff when asked (or offer to help if that’s the case), be awesome, etc.

@Professional Puppet (Active Member) - Be a member of the server for 2+ weeks, actively being personable and involved in server activities.

@Puppet (Member) - Remain in the server for 3+ days, and occasionally join in chats.

@Freshly Stuffed (New Member) - Join the server.

@Delinquent Toy (Punished Member) - Break the rules a certain amount of times. If a punished member continues breaking rules after joining this role, they may suffer more severe consequences.

Rules


1. Respect everyone. Hate speech or slander against any particular race, ethnicity, religion, gender, sexual identity, etc. is unacceptable. 2. No bullying. Harassment is not tolerated. This doesn’t that everywhere in the server is a “safe-space,” but if you’re attacking someone for no good reason then you’re out of line. 3. No porn/nudity. NSFW, 18+, and sensitive content are explicitly forbidden. It is punishable by ban on the first offense. 4. No excessive swearing. This does not mean that cuss words are forbidden, but use within reason. When in doubt, censor yourself. 5. No spam. This includes, but is not limited to, the following: sending 10+ consecutive messages in a row, sending the same link several times, sending the same image more than once, posting a topic in the incorrect chat room, etc. 6. No excessive role requests. I assure you, the more you ask, the less likely you are to obtain the requested role and the more likely you are to receive a downgrade or kick. 7. Speak in the appropriate channels. Examples: do not debate in the #general room; do not post Overwatch pics in the #server-requests room. 8. Do not argue with the staff. What we say goes. If we give you a warning, don’t sass the staff member who gave it. 9. Do not abuse your nickname privilege. If you want a nickname, it must be alphanumeric, or at the bare minimum the first 3 characters should be. 10. Do not start drama. If you’re intentionally causing server-wide pandemonium, you’ll be ejected immediately. 11. Do not exploit game chat rooms. If you are playing a game, such as Town of Salem, and you are cheating via means of discussing outside the game, that is a punishable offense. Note: Links to other servers are automatically deleted as a precaution against excessive advertising. If you would like to send a link to another server, let @System know.
